Task Manager is a tool used in operating systems, such as Windows, that allows users to view and manage the currently running processes and applications on their computer. It provides information about CPU and memory usage, disk activity, and network performance, as well as the ability to end or restart processes, view detailed performance graphs, and access other system-related features. Task Manager is useful for troubleshooting and monitoring the performance of a computer.

Remove task

Removing a task can bring a sense of relief and allow us to focus on more important and fulfilling responsibilities. It's essential to identify which tasks are unnecessary or no longer serve our goals and make the brave decision to remove them from our to-do list. By removing tasks, we create space for growth, productivity, and well-being.

Sort tasks by priority

Sorting tasks by priority is a smart and effective way to ensure that important and time-sensitive tasks are given the attention they deserve. By organizing tasks based on their urgency and importance, we can better allocate our resources and focus on completing the most critical tasks first. This approach minimizes procrastination and helps us stay on track to achieve our goals efficiently.

Marking a task as completed is an important step in any project or to-do list. It provides a sense of accomplishment and helps keep track of progress. It's satisfying to check off items from a list, knowing that they have been successfully finished. By marking tasks as completed, you can stay organized, motivated, and focused on the remaining tasks at hand.
